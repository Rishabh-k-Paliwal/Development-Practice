AI-Enabled Early Autism Screening & Doctor Assistance Platform

Multimodal AI system for early risk detection, diagnosis support & care continuity

Problem Statement

  • Autism diagnosis is often delayed due to limited specialists.
  • Early behavioral signs are subtle and easily missed.
  • Late intervention reduces long-term developmental outcomes.

Proposed 3-Module System

  • Module 1 – Objective Assessment
    Computer Vision analyzes eye contact, facial expressions, gestures, and attention patterns from child video data.
  • Module 2 – Subjective Assessment
    Structured parent observation questionnaires capture daily behavioral patterns and developmental concerns.
  • Module 3 – Combined Decision Module
    Fuses objective + subjective insights to generate explainable risk indicators for doctor assistance.

Ethical & Clinical Positioning: This system does not replace doctors. It acts as a decision-support tool to enable earlier screening, reduce diagnostic delays, and support personalized intervention planning with human-in-the-loop oversight.
